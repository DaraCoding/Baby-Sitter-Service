Fees

How Much We Charge Per Child



We charge $15 per child. $5 goes to charity, $5 is used for items in the Activity Box and $5 goes to the Baby-Sitter.







Charity



Charities are picked by the members of the club. The list for picking goes in alphabetical order. When all members have had a turn to pick the list starts over. The money you give us is partly used to support the people who realy need it.
